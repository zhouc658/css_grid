Experimental Camera

This filter is inspired by Sichuan opera's 'face-changing' art (变脸 bian lian). I designed the first mask based on a traditional mask you usually see during 'bian lian' as a sign of respect for this art. The other two masks are based on my favorite childhood characters: Monkey King and Kero (from Cardcaptor Sakura).
